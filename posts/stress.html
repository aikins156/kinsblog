STRESS

Many a time people do complain of being stressed out. What do they actually go through that they complain of stress? What makes them stressful? Are they really attributing stress to something else or it’s basically the stress they are going through? Stress is inevitable in our daily lives and as such when it happens we should not run away from it but find solutions to them so they do not have negative impacts on us.

Right from child birth through to death there are lots of stresses we go through. Some may include academic stress, occupational stress, relationship stress amongst others. Stress can also be referred to as Pressure,Tension,Anxiety,Worry,Distress.

Stress can be defined as a condition or a situation where demand on a person exceeds resources available for him to adequately respond to that demand.

There is no way a situation can result in stress but our reaction towards that situation causes the stress. Events that causes stress are called stressors and the effects of stress is Strain. Some causes of stress include; job pressure, money , health, poor nutrition, relationship many more.

People ask what really makes an event(stressor) very stressful?

1. When the event is unpredictable
2. When it involves a lot of pressure
3. When it is unavoidable
4. When it is intense
5. Finally, when it is unfamiliar.
Sit and relate these conditions to yourself and one situation you have been through. You would realize that one or two of these has a connection with what made you stressed out. Decisions or solutions to stress have been misunderstood by most people these days. They tend to use the wrong options to deal with their stress. Others too use the right channels. To deal with a stressed condition, one has to
1. Identify the source of stress
2. You accept the role you play in maintaining or creating your stress level.
Do not go by the wrong ways of dealing with stress as they tend to be temporary source of relief. Some of the unhealthy ways people use are; Smoking,Drinking too much alcohol,Overeating or under-eating, Sitting for hours in front of the TV or computer, Withdrawing from friends, family, and social activities, Using pills or hard drugs to relax, Sleeping too much, Procrastinating, Filling up every minute of the day to avoid facing problems, Taking out your stress on others (lashing out, angry outbursts and exhibiting physical violence) etc.
Just as they are several ways of dealing with stress wrongly , the positive ones are many. But note that different strategies work out for different people. There is no one way out to deal with a stressed situation. To deal with it, you can either change the situation or you can change your reaction.
Change the situation:
•Avoid the stressor.
•Alter the stressor.
•Change your reaction:
•Adapt to the stressor.
•Accept the stressor.
Practically,
Eat well
Exercise
Meditate
Connect socially
Relax and
Finally prioritize your task.
